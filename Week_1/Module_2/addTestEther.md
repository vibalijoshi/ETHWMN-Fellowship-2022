After Creation of your account, add some test ether in your wallet. Also you would require some ether for transactions. 


## Possible Options to add Ether
- https://faucet.goerli.mudit.blog/ (Recommended)
- https://goerli-faucet.slock.it/


### Mistakes
While adding test ether in my Meta Mask wallet, I was using the mainnet network. When I switched to goerli network, the ether was not transferred there. So be careful wht your network is.
